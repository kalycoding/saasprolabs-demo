## What The AI Agent Will Handle

### Phase 1 (Pilot Scope)
1. **Provider/Hospital Lookup**
   - "Is XYZ Hospital covered under my plan?"
   - "Which hospitals near me accept my HMO?"
   - "What's the address/contact for ABC Clinic?"

2. **Plan Coverage Inquiries**
   - "Does my plan cover dental?"
   - "What's my coverage limit for prescriptions?"
   - "Am I covered for specialist visits?"

3. **Claims Status Check**
   - "What's the status of my claim?"
   - "When will my claim be processed?"
   - "Why was my claim rejected?"

4. **Basic Enrollment Questions**
   - "How do I enroll?"
   - "What documents do I need?"
   - "What plans are available?"

### Phase 2 (Post-Pilot Expansion)
- Pre-authorization requests
- Appointment scheduling with network providers
- Outbound calls for renewal reminders
- Claims submission via voice
